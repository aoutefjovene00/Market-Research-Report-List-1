<p><p>The Smart LED Lighting market is primarily divided into wired and wireless systems. Wired smart lighting utilizes electrical wiring to connect fixtures, offering reliable connectivity and consistent power supply, making it ideal for permanent installations. In contrast, wireless smart lighting uses technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or Zigbee, enabling flexibility and easier installation without extensive cabling. Wireless systems allow for remote control and integration with smart home systems, appealing to consumers looking for convenience and energy efficiency.</p></p>
