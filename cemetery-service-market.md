<p><p>Cemetery service markets consist of two main types: At-Need and Pre-Need. In contrast, Pre-Need services involve planning and purchasing burial plots or services in advance, allowing individuals to organize their end-of-life preferences ahead of time. This proactive approach alleviates emotional stress on families during difficult times and often includes financial arrangements to lock in current prices.</p></p>
