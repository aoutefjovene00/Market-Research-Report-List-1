<p><strong>The Liquid Chromatography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid-Liquid Chromatography (LLC)</li><li>Liquid-Solid Chromatography (LSC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid chromatography systems market can be categorized primarily into Liquid-Liquid Chromatography (LLC) and Liquid-Solid Chromatography (LSC). LLC utilizes a non-polar liquid stationary phase and an aqueous mobile phase, ideal for separating compounds based on their solubility in different solvents. In contrast, LSC employs a solid stationary phase and a liquid mobile phase, effectively separating analytes based on their interaction with the stationary material. Both methods are essential for purifying and analyzing complex mixtures in various scientific applications.</p></p>
