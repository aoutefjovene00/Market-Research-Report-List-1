<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Form-Fill-Seal (FFS) packaging is categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical FFS involves packaging materials that are fed vertically into the machine, suitable for producing stand-up pouches and bags from rolls of flexible film. This method is ideal for high-speed operations with smaller products. In contrast, horizontal FFS involves horizontally oriented machines that fill and seal products in trays or pouches, often used for larger items or bulk packaging, providing efficient production with enhanced filling capabilities.</p></p>
