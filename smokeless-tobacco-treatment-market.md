<p><p>The smokeless tobacco treatment market encompasses various types, including chewing tobacco, snuff/dipping tobacco, and dissolvable tobacco. Chewing tobacco involves loose-leaf products placed between the gums and cheeks, while snuff is finely ground tobacco, available in dry or moist forms, used by placing it in the mouth or sniffing it. Dissolvable tobacco comes in forms like strips or pellets that dissolve in the mouth. Each type presents unique challenges for cessation, driving demand for targeted treatments and interventions to aid users in quitting.</p></p>
